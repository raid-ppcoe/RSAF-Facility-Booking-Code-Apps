Clearance Booking Workflow

Step-by-step process for booking facilities that require security clearance through Force Protection (FP) on OSN.

How It Works

Four clear steps from booking to approval. Your slot is reserved throughout.

Step 1
Submit Booking
User fills in booking details and submits a request for a clearance-required facility.
Step 2
Email Clearance to FP
User emails clearance details to Force Protection on OSN, CC the facility approver.
Click to view email
Step 3
Clearance Approved
Force Protection reviews and confirms the clearance request.
Step 4
Booking Approved
Facility approver gives final approval. Booking is confirmed.
Your booking slot is reserved from the moment you submit — no one else can book the same time, even before final approval.

Booking Statuses

Each booking moves through these stages. Only rejected/cancelled bookings release the time slot.

Pending
Awaiting approver review
Processing Clearance
Clearance email sent to FP
Clearance Processed
FP has confirmed clearance
Approved
Booking confirmed & active
Rejected / Cancelled
Slot released for others

Reservation Guarantee

The system blocks the timeslot as soon as a booking is submitted. Conflict detection ensures no double-bookings at any stage of the clearance process.

  • Pending bookings block the slot
  • Processing clearance bookings block the slot
  • Only rejected/cancelled releases it

What You Need to Email

Include the following details when emailing Force Protection (FP) on OSN for clearance:

  • Full NRIC
  • Rank / Name
  • Unit
  • Hp No.
  • Key No.: Key 35 (TAH)
  • Date
  • Time
  • Purpose

Clearance Email Template

To Force Protection (FP) on OSN
CC Facility Approver
Subject Clearance Request — [Facility Name] — [Date]
Message Body
Dear FP,

I would like to request clearance for the following booking. Please find my details below:
Full NRIC [Your full NRIC]
Rank / Name [Your rank and name]
Unit [Your unit]
Hp No. [Your handphone number]
Key No. Key 35 (TAH)
Date [Booking date]
Time [Booking time]
Purpose [Booking purpose]
Send this email on OSN. Your booking slot is reserved while clearance is being processed.
